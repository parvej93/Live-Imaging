Environmental chamber — control circuit

Reconstructed from wiring photos, July 14 2026 · Arduino Uno R3 as hub

Yellow — D13, K30 Rx Orange — D12, K30 Tx Teal 1 — D11, heat-enable bus Teal 2 — D8, NTC divider Brown — D9, valve enable Blue — A0, NTC read Red — 5V / 12V power Black — GND

5V and GND are common-tied across the Arduino, both controller boards' reference terminals, the 12V supply's return, and the NTC divider's low leg — that's why they're shown as a single dot + label on each board rather than long traced wires. Teal 1 and Teal 2 are the same wire color used for two different signals (D11's heat-enable bus and D8's divider tap) — kept visually identical here since that's what they are on the bench.
